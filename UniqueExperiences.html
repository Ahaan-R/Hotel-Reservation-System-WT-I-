Unique Experiences

One Hell of An Experience

La-Casa Experiences

Serene surroundings. Luxurious rooms. And legendary service. A stay at La-Casa Hotels promises all of these benefits and more. The next time you visit, you could leave with something even more memorable - a La-Casa Experience. So as you make your reservation with us, give our menu of experiences a close look. With personalised options for each destination, you could well have a La-Casa Experience that stays with you long after your holiday ends.

An Elephant safari to Naila

Travel like Royalty. Enjoy and explore the landscape of the Jaipur countryside with a leisurely elephant ride that culminates with a gourmet picnic at the magnificently restored Naila Fort. The safari can be arranged either early in the morning or in the late afternoon.

Duration : 4 hours

INR: 19000 for a couple

Applicable taxes are extra

Reservations required.

Renewal of Vows

Nothing could be more romantic than renewing your marriage vows in a true Hindu ceremony. A day of pampering for the lady with our special beauty therapy, the 'Romance of Rajputana.' Embellish your hands with Mehndi, a form of traditional Indian body art. Get an exclusive Indian hairstyle for the special evening. The gentleman can indulge in a special spa treatment, The Rajvilās Classic, that will leave you feeling rejuvenated and ready for the memorable evening

Duration : Half Day

INR: 22000 for a couple

Applicable taxes are extra

Chaarbagh dinner

Indulge in a Rajasthani Thali meal overlooking the pool and an ancient haveli (mansion) on one side and the 280 year old Shiva temple on the other. Lit up with mashaals (gas torches) and charmingly decorated with earthen lamps and flowers, this is a magnificent setting.

Duration 2 hours

INR: 15000 for a couple

Applicable taxes are extra

Cooking sessions

Put on your apron and turn into a chef for a special culinary session. Learn the secrets of herbs and spices used in Indian cuisine. Try your hand at authentic recipes of Rajasthan like Lal Maas or simple dishes like Rajvilās Chicken Curry or Dal. Take home the recipes of the dishes and an apron with The Oberoi insignia.

Duration 2 hours

INR: 5000 per person

Applicable taxes are extra
